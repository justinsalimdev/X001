My Narrative

I'm Justin Salim, a second-year student at KCG (Kyoto Computer Gakuin), driven by the fusion of Web/App Development and Artificial Intelligence (AI). I established this portfolio to document my technical growth and showcase my projects. My commitment is to continuous learning, disciplined practice, and applying my knowledge to build useful digital solutions.

My coding journey began in **2022**. Currently, I'm balancing full-time studies with part-time work to support my life in Kyoto, dedicating my focus to coding, design, and personal development (including Buddhism). This balance ensures strong practical work experience alongside technical learning.

My primary goal is deep proficiency in full-stack development, achieving mastery in frameworks like React.js, and exploring strategies for project monetization, such as AdSense. I am eager to collaborate on challenging projects that accelerate my growth.
